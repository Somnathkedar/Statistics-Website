What is Statistics?

Statistics is the science of collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data. It helps us understand information and make decisions based on facts rather than guesses.

Why is Statistics Important?

Statistics is used in education, business, medicine, government, and research. It helps students analyze exam results, researchers study data, and companies make decisions.

Types of Statistics

Statistics is mainly divided into descriptive statistics and inferential statistics. Descriptive statistics summarizes data, while inferential statistics helps draw conclusions.

Real-Life Example

If the average marks of a class are calculated, statistics helps summarize performance without checking each student individually.
