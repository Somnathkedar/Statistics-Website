What is Biostatistics?

Biostatistics applies statistical methods to biology, medicine, and public health research. It helps analyze medical data and supports evidence-based decisions.

Role of Biostatistics

Biostatistics helps design experiments, analyze medical data, evaluate treatments, and improve healthcare outcomes.

Clinical Trials

Clinical trials test new drugs and treatments. Biostatisticians analyze trial data to ensure safety and effectiveness.

Career Opportunities

Biostatistics offers careers such as biostatistician, clinical data analyst, and research analyst.
