Descriptive Statistics

Descriptive statistics is used to summarize and describe data in a meaningful way. It converts raw data into simple numbers, tables, and graphs.

Measures of Central Tendency

Mean is the average value, median is the middle value, and mode is the most frequent value.

Measures of Dispersion

Dispersion shows how spread out the data is. Common measures include range, variance, and standard deviation.

Graphical Representation

Bar charts, pie charts, histograms, and line graphs are used to visualize data clearly.
